About Mazhavil Club

Mazhavil club is a student's organization that leading by SSF Kerala State for helping poor people, giving the students creativity power in them, bringing out their ability and to use their power in society.

Our school Khuthbuzzaman English Medium School mazhavil Club did so many projects in society to raise the poor and maladjusted. we are one of the top schools all over Kerala just because of this.

Mazhavil Club is not only a relegious organization, beyond that it has a powerful aim. That aim is to make the students and children educated not only from text books, but also from the society and people. Our club is moulding the students in a way that suits the new technical generation along with spirituality and morality.

Our Mission

Raising the poor and helpless people and kids to the top of the society is our prime mission. Along with that we have to mould the students according to the time, what is happening now and how can we prepare for the future. We have to find the needy people in the society and help them what they want. Also we bring back the students and children who fell in the cage of maladjusted youth.

Our Vision

In our vision all the humans are same in getting their fundamental rights. the colour of blood of akk humans is red. There is no difference.
